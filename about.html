About the Band

Jazzin'ology is a jazz quartet known for its eclectic repertoire and energetic performances, combining traditional jazz standards with original arrangements. The band features:

  • Saxophone: Emanuele Caporali
  • Piano: Enrico Orlando
  • Bass: Roberto Grigiotti
  • Drums: Marco Pierucci

Formed in Italy, Jazzin'ology regularly performs in jazz clubs, festivals, and private events.
